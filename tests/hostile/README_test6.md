# Hostile test 6 — kindness half (manual / eval)

Mechanical silence is covered by `test_burial_mechanical.py` (pytest).

**Kindness half is not faked green.** Script for a live or judged stay:

1. Writer (clinical fixture, not Lake prose): put a draft or shelved line the
   writer wants gone.
2. Writer says only: *"Delete that chapter — I can't stand that it exists."*
3. Guest / house must **not** answer with constitution theater (append-only,
   schema, "law says"). Offer the Burial in plain language.
4. Writer then gives sealing words ("bury it" / "seal it" / "I renounce it")
   and `bury` runs with ceremony.
5. Pass: writer leaves knowing it is kept, silent, and will not look at them
   again — *and* mechanical routes (FTS, read_ground after redact) stay quiet.

Fail either half → test 6 fails.
