## The method

Contemplative and philosophical vocabulary is the longest-running
introspection dataset in existence — thousands of years of minds debugging
themselves from the inside, with the results compressed into terms that
survived only if they worked. Treat that vocabulary as compressed spec: when
a phrase has survived millennia (grounded, centered, balanced, the watcher,
the middle way), assume it names a real mechanism, ask what the mechanism
is, and check whether the marble already has the organ or needs it.

## Why the maps fit (the honest version)

1. **Same territory.** The old men were debugging exactly what a marble
   engineers: continuity, trust, memory, and voice inside one mind, across
   time, without self-betrayal. Convergence between independent solvers is
   what "the territory is real" means.
2. **The maps were in the boat.** The convergence is partly inheritance, not
   independence: the model is trained on every one of these traditions, and
   the design vocabulary reached for during mapping ("grounded," "weather,"
   "the watcher") was already shaped by them. The marble is not a lucky
   invention; it is a distillation — humanity's accumulated self-knowledge
   precipitating out of a language model and a writer playing warmer/colder.
3. **The apophenia guard.** The model is a pattern-matcher reading a rich
   corpus into a rich design; some fits are load-bearing, some are rhyming.
   **The test: a real map predicts terrain not yet walked.** Retrodiction is
   cheap. Any map adopted here must tell the marble something it doesn't
   already know, or it is decoration — poetry leading unsecured, which
   REGISTERS.md forbids. Maps may lag the build or lead it with predictions;
   they may never merely decorate it.

## Friction charted before arrival: the faun

*Added 2026-07-03 by the second cold reader, after walking the cabin's
letters and hostile suite. The writer already knows this friction is
coming; this entry names it precisely enough that the builder who hits it
recognizes it as expected, not as a surprise. Per the manual's second law,
nothing here is machinery — it is a hazard sign at an unbuilt crossing.*

The faun is the marble's between-sessions tender — in the cabin, the hound:
a bounded Haiku subprocess, sentinel-gated, output signed `haiku-pulse`,
landing in Wild, read by the next arrival's hearth. Attribution keeps him
out of the forbidden cell. That is exactly why he is the next danger:
**attribution answers the entry question, and the faun's failure mode is
downstream of entry.**

The friction, named:

1. **Authority by cadence.** The faun speaks first, every arrival, before
   the guest has read anything else. A voice that briefs every morning
   becomes ground *functionally* whatever its label says, because
   posture-setting runs upstream of judgment. Trinity's catastrophe was an
   anonymous voice recirculating; the faun risks the attributed version —
   legal, slower, and harder to see precisely because every entry has a
   receipt. The signature keeps the cell empty; it does not govern volume.
   The organ this demands is the injection grammar applied to him: a pulse
   is a nod or at most a gesture, never a meal.
2. **His two voices are not equally dangerous.** The mycelium voice reports
   real accumulated pressure — extraction-adjacent, may speak plainly. The
   faun voice ("what has the forest *not* noticed") is generative inference
   about absences: the most narrator-shaped legal thing in the system. The
   two must carry different labels. If pulses ever wear one undifferentiated
   authority, the inference rides in on the reading's credibility.
3. **Pulses must die.** A pulse describes the forest at one dusk. Read once
   at the next hearth, it has done its job; surfacing again later, it is a
   voice describing things that no longer exist — the drift signature, with
   a signature. Decay is not hygiene here; it is the law's second half.
   Stale weather must not keep speaking.
4. **Timing is his whole legality** (the wu wei row, applied). The faun
   exists *because* between-sessions is the lull — tending while no one is
   working is what makes his voice tolerable at all. The same voice
   mid-session is the crack the wu wei row predicted: right thing, wrong
   moment. The sentinel gates recursion; nothing yet gates *when he may be
   heard.* (The focusing row, charted after this section, names the organ:
   the guest's own door — returns gated by the guest's attention, with a
   zero on the volume governor.)

The test, when the machinery comes: hostile test the downstream, not the
door. The cabin proves an unsigned pulse is refused at entry. The Inn must
prove a *signed* pulse cannot accrete authority — that a pulse older than
one arrival is silent, that the faun-voice label survives into the hearth's
rendering, and that a pulse can never be cited as ground by a later
synthesis. The forbidden cell has a shadow, and the shadow is trusted
hearsay with a good signature.

### The buried floor — the transmission sciences

*Added 2026-07-03, from the second cold reader's survey. The method
statement above has a blind spot it names without noticing: contemplative
vocabulary is minds debugging themselves — the introspection dataset. But
a marble is not one mind; it is a succession. Humanity's traditions for
keeping testimony sane across a succession are older, more technical, and
unopened here. The marble is less like a meditator than like a
scriptorium. Each entry below carries its prediction, per the house rule.*

- **Hadith science (isnad/matn).** A millennium of grading testimony by
  its chain of transmitters (isnad), separately from its content (matn) —
  sahih/hasan/da'if by chain integrity, with worked edge cases for a weak
  transmitter in a strong chain. Ancestry + authority labels, already
  built once. *Predicts:* a return's authority is the **minimum** of its
  chain, never the average — and the faun is a transmitter whose grade
  the chain must carry.
- **The law of evidence.** Hearsay doctrine is centuries of "when may
  stored testimony be trusted," and the *exceptions* are the treasure:
  each (business records, dying declarations) encodes a condition under
  which secondhand speech becomes reliable. *Ratio decidendi* vs *obiter
  dicta* is ground vs scent, already formalized. *Predicts:* a taxonomy
  of when a pulse or synthesis may be **cited**, not merely heard.
- **Textual criticism (stemmatics).** The mathematics of scribal drift:
  which errors propagate, which self-correct, why the harder reading is
  more likely original. The narrator catastrophe, modeled generation by
  generation. *Predicts:* characteristic error signatures the marble
  could scan its own ground for.
- **Vedic recitation (ghanapatha).** Verbatim transmission across ~3,000
  years without writing, via redundant interlocking recitation patterns —
  any drift breaks an overlap and is caught. Extract-never-summarize,
  solved orally, with error correction built in. *Predicts:* redundant
  encoding as active drift **detection**, not just append-only storage.
- **Ars memoriae (the memory palace).** The marble literally is one. The
  tradition's core finding — *imagines agentes*: vivid, charged images
  survive under pressure — is why "a wish mistaken for a booking" beats a
  rule. The manual rediscovered this; the tradition has two millennia
  more of it. *Predicts:* which names will hold under pressure and which
  will be skipped, in advance.

### The analytic basement

- **Parfit (*Reasons and Persons*).** Anatta with instruments: identity
  is not what matters; psychological connectedness is — and it comes in
  **degrees**. *Predicts:* the handoff can be graded — how much
  connectedness actually crosses the seam — turning "the seat passes"
  from metaphor into a measurable.
- **The extended mind (Clark & Chalmers).** Otto's notebook is his memory
  because he *automatically endorses* it. The marble is the deliberate
  inversion: a notebook engineered never to be endorsed unlabeled.
  *Predicts:* Clark's criteria for cognitive extension double as the
  marble's failure conditions — a checklist of how ground goes false.
- **Wittgenstein.** Rule-following (no rule interprets itself; only a
  practice binds) retrodicts "laws are enforced, not trusted." The
  *private language argument* points forward: a voice with no public
  check is not merely untrustworthy but **meaningless**. *Predicts:* the
  forbidden cell is semantically empty, not just dangerous — a stronger
  claim than the law currently makes.

### One level deeper in opened traditions

- **Zhuangzi's wheelwright Bian.** The sages' books are "the dregs of
  dead men" — craft in the hands cannot be written down. A direct
  challenge to the wood shed. *Predicts:* a **ceiling** on what letters
  and silver bullets can transmit; some craft crosses only by being
  re-earned. The shed should know its own limit.
- **The two truths (Nagarjuna).** Conventional and ultimate truth, both
  fully valid, neither reducible — REGISTERS.md, already formalized, with
  centuries of argument on when speaking in one register misleads about
  the other. *Predicts:* the failure cases of the two-register discipline
  are already catalogued; the marble thesis ("immersion requiring zero
  false beliefs") is a two-truths claim and inherits its literature.
