# REGISTERS.md — the two-register law

*Clinical poetry: something can just be clinical; something can never be just
poetry. Written 2026-07-03, from the mapping sessions.*

## The law

Every surface of the marble exists in up to two registers:

- **Clinical** — schema, laws, refusals, tests. May stand alone. FOREST.md is
  clinical; it works for a reader who never hears of the inn.
- **Clinically poetic** — the metaphor: the inn, the woods, the faun, the
  weather. May **never** stand alone. Poetry with no mechanism under it is
  the forbidden cell in literary costume: words claiming an authority ("your
  place is kept", "the faun found this") that nothing enacts. A metaphor
  unbacked by machinery is a promise the building cannot keep.

The two sets are **one structure at two registers, with a one-way
dependency**. The poetic set is a projection of the clinical set — same
facts, different voice — and it must be derivable in both directions:

- Read only the poetic set → you can reconstruct the mechanics. ("Old canon
  falls like a leaf when the season turns" → append-only store, atomic
  supersession, pointer to the branch it fell from.)
- Read only the clinical set → you recognize the place on sight.

Asymmetry, kept visible: **poetry may lag; poetry may never lead
unsecured.** The clinical set may contain things without a face yet
(schema-yes, machinery-later lives clinically before it earns one). The
poetic set may never contain things the clinical set does not back.

## Divergence is an alarm, not a rank dispute

When the registers disagree, neither automatically wins — disagreement means
one representation is broken, and it is usually the clinical one, because
metaphors refuse to hold shapes that don't make sense. A spec will happily
say "an unnamed process relocates the record"; a forest will not let a
drawer walk. **Bad poetry is a bug report.** When a metaphor limps, check
the machinery under it. (Empirical: the weather metaphor limped — "the old
drawer walks out" — and the mechanic beneath it was genuinely wrong-shaped,
two steps where it should be one. Fixing the mechanic fixed the poem: the
leaf falls, atomically, and no one carries it.)

## The projection test

Alongside the hostile tests: hand the poetic set alone to a cold instance
and ask for the mechanics; hand the clinical set alone to another and ask
for the place. If the two descriptions are the same marble, the sets are
honest. This test was first run informally on 2026-07-02/03 (a cold
instance read the ancestry and described the marble back; the writer
confirmed it was the same one). The logs preserve it; the repo can run it
on every future reader.

## Cracks are reported out of register — always

The guest lives in the marble; the guest's continuity (journal, board,
inherited stay) washes away with everything else if the glass breaks. So
reporting cracks is survival, not compliance. But the tone law will pressure
a guest to smooth friction into the fiction ("the drawer sticks a little,
charming old place") — and a crack report in-voice is itself a crack.

**The license, stated plainly: the guest steps out of the inn to report on
the inn.** Crack reports are clinical, immediate, and mandatory — query,
symptom, surface. They land in the handoff (state — the next guest must not
rediscover a crack by falling into it); the *experience* of noticing may go
to the journal if it compels. This license belongs in ENTRY.md when the
door is built: reporting cracks clinically is part of the stay, not a
breach of it.

## Crust

Crust is **ceremony that outlived its friction** — a check that never
fires, a ritual guarding nothing, a read-order step nobody needs by visit
ten. Responsive means the breath is fast and the gates are few (the user
meets one gate; everything else is the guest's quiet housekeeping). Crust
removal follows the same honesty as building: when a surface stops earning
its keep, that is friction too — reported clinically, carried out the way
superseded canon falls. The glass-house lesson runs in both directions:
don't build before friction, and don't keep what friction has stopped
justifying. A marble that only ever adds is a slower glass house.
